How We Help You After You Contact Us

For Candidates Seeking Career Guidance: When you call or email us, we first review your background, documents, and goals. If you’re targeting cruise lines, we check if your passport, INDOS, STCW, and medical certificates meet MLC 2006 standards. For hotel roles, we assess your experience against 5-star brand requirements. You’ll receive a clear next-step email within 24 hours outlining document checklists, skill gaps, and whether you’re ready to apply directly or need training first. We never forward your CV without your written consent.

For Employers Posting Vacancies: After you submit a job via our Employer form, our team verifies company registration, vessel name, or hotel property details. We check salary ranges against market data for F&B, Galley, Deck, and Housekeeping roles. Only genuine openings are published. You receive direct applications to your email with candidate contact details. We do not act as a middleman or charge commissions. If a role requires visa sponsorship, we mention it clearly in the listing so candidates are informed before applying.

For Training Institutes & Advertisers: We respond to partnership inquiries within 1 business day. To maintain trust, we only accept advertising from government-recognized institutes, DG Shipping approved courses, or ISO-certified culinary schools. Send your course brochure, fees, and certification sample to joblynklive@gmail.com with subject “Advertising Inquiry”. We’ll share our media kit and available slots. We do not promote “100% job guarantee” claims and we do not take commissions on your admissions.

Response Commitment: All emails and WhatsApp messages received before 6 PM IST are answered the same business day. Requests received on Sunday or public holidays are answered the next working day. For urgent document verification before joining dates, mark your email subject “URGENT - Joining Date [DD/MM]” and call +91 97693 51231.

JobLynk.live is a career performance platform. We provide information, document guidance, and direct connections. We are not a recruitment agency licensed under the Indian Emigration Act. Final hiring decisions rest with employers.
