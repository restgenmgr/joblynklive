SQL Filtering Data – WHERE, AND, OR, IN, BETWEEN

📅 April 5, 2026 · 10 min read

Filter data using WHERE, AND, OR, IN, and BETWEEN. Essential for real-world queries.

WHERE with Comparison Operators

SELECT * FROM employees WHERE salary > 60000;

AND / OR

SELECT * FROM employees WHERE department = 'IT' AND salary > 70000;

IN

SELECT * FROM employees WHERE department IN ('IT', 'Marketing');

BETWEEN

SELECT * FROM employees WHERE salary BETWEEN 55000 AND 75000;
