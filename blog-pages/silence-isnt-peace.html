Silence Isn't Peace. It's a Slow Poison.

📅 April 5, 2026 · 3 min read

Clarification is an emotional duty. Don't let unspoken words kill a strong bond.

When you choose silence over a difficult conversation, you leave the other person with unanswered questions.

  • "Did I do something wrong?"
  • "Was our bond never that strong?"
  • "Why the sudden wall of ice?"

If the relationship matters, choose vulnerability over silence. Talk it out. Clarify. Repair.
