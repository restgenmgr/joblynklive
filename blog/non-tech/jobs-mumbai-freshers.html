Jobs in Mumbai for Freshers – Complete Guide 2026

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, offers thousands of job opportunities for freshers across multiple industries. Whether you are a recent graduate or someone looking for your first job, Mumbai has options in retail, hospitality, BPO, logistics, and administration.

Top Industries Hiring Freshers in Mumbai

1. Retail Jobs

Retail stores, malls, and showrooms regularly hire freshers for roles like sales associates, store assistants, and cashiers.

2. Hospitality Jobs

Hotels, cafes, and restaurants are constantly looking for staff in roles such as stewards, baristas, and front desk executives.

3. BPO & Customer Service

Call centers and customer support roles are ideal for freshers with good communication skills.

4. Delivery & Logistics

Companies like courier services and e-commerce platforms offer flexible jobs for freshers.

Skills Required for Fresher Jobs

  • Basic communication skills
  • Positive attitude
  • Willingness to learn
  • Time management

Salary Expectations

Freshers in Mumbai can expect salaries ranging from ₹12,000 to ₹25,000 per month depending on the role and industry.
