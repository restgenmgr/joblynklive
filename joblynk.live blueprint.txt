JobLynk Landing Page HTML Code
JobLynk.live is a hybrid AI–human career performance platform designed to measure and improve interview readiness using structured evaluation metrics.

It is NOT a job board.
It does not list jobs.
It focuses on candidate preparation and measurable performance improvement before applications are submitted.

The platform includes the following core components:

Resume Analysis Engine
Users upload their resume.
The system evaluates:

ATS compatibility

Keyword alignment with target roles

Structural clarity

Impact strength of bullet points

The system generates a resume score and improvement recommendations.

Mock Interview Simulation
Users select a target job role.
The system generates structured interview questions.
Users respond via text (initial MVP).
The system evaluates:

Clarity

Structure

Relevance

Outcome orientation

Job Performance Index (JPI)
A composite readiness score calculated from:

Resume score

Interview simulation score

Role alignment percentage

The JPI reflects the candidate’s overall interview readiness level.

Performance Badge
Candidates who meet predefined performance thresholds receive a shareable digital readiness badge.

Tiered Pricing Model
Basic Plan – Resume Scan
Smart Growth – Resume + Interview + JPI
Pro Acceleration – Full system + badge qualification

Assurance Policy
The platform guarantees measurable improvement in resume alignment and interview performance scores, with conditional re-optimization support.

Target Users:

Graduates entering workforce

Professionals with 3–12 years experience

Candidates preparing for career advancement

Primary Goal:
Increase interview conversion probability by improving structured preparation.

Tone of the application:
Professional, data-driven, precise, performance-focused.
