Why Customer Service is a Smart Choice

Customer service teaches you how a business really works – from the customer’s perspective. Many executives started here.

Key Skills

  • Communication
  • Problem-solving
  • Patience & EQ
  • Basic computer skills

Salary (India 2026)

  • Entry: ₹2.5–4 LPA
  • Experienced: ₹4–7 LPA
  • Team Leader: ₹7–12 LPA
  • Manager: ₹12–20 LPA+

Where to Apply

Indeed, Naukri, LinkedIn, company career pages (Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, Razorpay), BPOs (Concentrix, Wipro, Teleperformance).

Career Growth

  1. Associate
  2. Senior Associate
  3. Team Leader
  4. Manager
  5. Director of Customer Experience

Tips to Get Hired

  • Tailor your resume to highlight problem-solving
  • Learn basic CRM (Zendesk, Freshdesk)
  • Get a customer service certification
