And in 2026, Mumbai remains India's top hub for affordable, DG Shipping approved STCW training.

What Is STCW Basic Safety Training?

STCW Basic Safety Training — also called STCW BST or STCW 2010 Manila Amendments — is a set of four mandatory safety courses required by the International Maritime Organization (IMO) for anyone working onboard a ship. The four modules are:

  • Personal Survival Techniques (PST) — Liferaft deployment, survival at sea, use of lifejackets and immersion suits.
  • Fire Prevention and Fire Fighting (FPFF) — Fire prevention, use of firefighting equipment, emergency drills.
  • Elementary First Aid (EFA) — Basic CPR, wound care, patient stabilization at sea.
  • Personal Safety and Social Responsibilities (PSSR) — Ship safety culture, emergency procedures, onboard communication.

All four are bundled together and referred to as the STCW Basic Safety Training package. In Mumbai, this is offered as a 5–7 day residential or non-residential course at DG Shipping approved Maritime Training Institutes (MTIs).

DG Shipping Approved STCW Institutes in Mumbai

Only institutes approved by the Directorate General of Shipping (DG Shipping), Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways, Government of India are recognized for issuing valid STCW certificates. Here are the well-known DG Shipping approved Maritime Training Institutes (MTIs) operating in and around Mumbai in 2026:

  • Anglo-Eastern Maritime Academy (AEMA) — Belapur, Navi Mumbai. One of India's largest and most reputed MTIs. Offers full BST and advanced STCW courses.
  • Tolani Maritime Institute (TMI) — Pune (approx 3 hrs from Mumbai). Highly regarded, residential campus with excellent facilities.
  • International Maritime Institute (IMI) — Greater Noida. Offers Mumbai-based training programs periodically.
  • Samundra Institute of Maritime Studies (SIMS) — Lonavala, near Mumbai. DG Shipping approved, residential, well-equipped simulation facilities.
  • Maharashtra Academy of Naval Education and Training (MANET) — Pune. Affordable and DG Shipping approved for STCW BST courses.
  • Bombay Marine Academy — Mumbai. Centrally located, popular with Mumbai-based freshers for affordability and convenience.
  • Vidyalankar Maritime Institute — Wadala, Mumbai. Budget-friendly option for STCW BST near central Mumbai.

Always verify the current DG Shipping approval status of any institute before enrolling by checking the official DG Shipping website at dgshipping.gov.in. Approval statuses are updated periodically and some smaller institutes lose recognition.

STCW for Freshers in Mumbai: Can You Get Certified Without Experience?

Yes — absolutely. The STCW Basic Safety Training course has no minimum sea service requirement. Any Indian national above 18 years of age with a Class 10 pass certificate can enroll. This makes it the perfect entry-level qualification for freshers looking to start a maritime or cruise ship career in 2026 without prior experience.

Here is the typical step-by-step process for freshers in Mumbai:

  • Obtain an Indos Number (Indian National Database of Seafarers) from DG Shipping online — free of charge.
  • Apply for a Seafarer's Identity Document (SID) or CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) — required for employment.
  • Enroll in a DG Shipping approved institute for the STCW BST package.
  • Complete the course (5–7 days), pass assessments, and receive your STCW certificate.
  • For cruise ship hospitality roles, additionally complete Crowd Management and CMHB courses.
  • Apply through cruise line recruitment agencies or directly to cruise companies.
⚠️ Important: Avoid Fake STCW Certificates

A number of unrecognized institutes in Mumbai and across India offer cheap STCW certificates that are not DG Shipping approved. These are invalid for employment and may lead to rejection at port authority checks. Always verify the institute's approval number on the DG Shipping portal before paying any fees.

How Long Does STCW Certification Take?

The STCW BST package is completed in 5 to 7 consecutive days at most Mumbai institutes. Some institutes offer weekend-only batches spread across 2–3 weekends for working professionals. Certificates are typically issued within 7–14 working days after course completion and are valid for 5 years, after which refresher courses are mandatory under STCW 2010 regulations.

Is STCW Worth It for Cruise Ship Jobs in 2026?

Without question. A cruise ship hospitality or F&B role for an Indian national can pay between USD 800 – USD 2,500 per month tax-free, with free accommodation, food, and travel. An investment of ₹25,000–₹45,000 in STCW certification can be recovered within the first month of employment. For freshers in Mumbai with no prior experience, STCW is the single most impactful career investment available in 2026.
