Privacy Policy for JobLynk.live

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

JobLynk.live uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized information about site usage. This includes pages visited, time spent on pages, referring websites, and general geographic location at the city level. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily submit them through our contact form or employer inquiry form.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate essential site features, analyze traffic patterns, and deliver relevant advertisements. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

JobLynk.live displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.

Google's use of advertising cookies, including the DART cookie, enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to JobLynk.live and other websites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

You may also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

4. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is automatically deleted after 26 months. Information submitted through forms is retained for 24 months for business record-keeping purposes, then securely deleted.

5. Third-Party Links

JobLynk.live contains links to external websites including cruise lines, hotel brands, and job boards. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

6. Children's Privacy

JobLynk.live is intended for professional career development and is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
