💰 Refund Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2025

1. General Policy

At JobLynk.live, we strive to provide high-quality career services including resume writing, cover letter creation, and HR lead data. Due to the digital nature of our products and services, refunds are handled on a conditional basis as outlined below.

2. Eligibility for Refund (Conditional)

Refunds may be issued under the following conditions:

  • Service Not Delivered: If we fail to deliver your resume/cover letter within 5 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays), you may request a full refund.
  • Quality Issue: If the delivered service does not meet the basic quality standards or contains factual errors not caused by incorrect information provided by you, we will revise it free of charge. If revision does not resolve the issue, a partial refund (up to 50%) may be issued.
  • Duplicate Payment: If you are charged twice for the same package, we will refund the duplicate amount immediately.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after service has been delivered
  • Incorrect information provided by you (wrong email, wrong phone number, etc.)
  • Failure to respond to our requests for information within 7 days
  • Dissatisfaction with job placement outcomes (we provide tools and leads, not guaranteed jobs)
  • HR lead data: once delivered, these are non-refundable as they are immediately usable information

4. Refund Request Process

  1. Email us at joblynklive@gmail.com with subject line: "REFUND REQUEST – [Order ID/Phone Number]"
  2. Provide the date of purchase, payment method, and reason for refund
  3. We will respond within 3 business days
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without first requesting a refund, we reserve the right to dispute the chargeback and permanently suspend your access to our services.
