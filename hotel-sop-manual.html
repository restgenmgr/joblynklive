📋 Hotel Standard Operating Procedures

Professional Standards Manual | 8 Core Departments

🏨 Front Office Department

  • Check-in: 5 minutes max
  • Call answering: within 3 rings
  • Complaint resolution: 15 minutes
KPIs: GSS, Check-in Time, Upsell Rate

🧹 Housekeeping Department

  • Standard room: 25 minutes
  • Deep cleaning: 45 minutes
  • Final inspection before ready
KPIs: Inspection Score, Turnaround Time

🍽️ Food & Beverage

  • Greet within 1 minute
  • Order within 5 minutes
  • Food delivery: 15-20 min
KPIs: Table Turnover, Food Cost %

👨‍🍳 Kitchen Department

  • Standardized recipes
  • Portion control
  • Temperature logs
KPIs: Food Quality, Waste %

🔧 Maintenance

  • Daily inspections
  • Critical: Immediate
  • Low priority: 24 hours
KPIs: Downtime, Response Time

🛡️ Security

  • 24/7 surveillance
  • Fire: Evacuate
  • Medical: Call emergency
KPIs: Incident Response, Safety Audit

👥 Human Resources

  • Recruitment SOP
  • Background verification
  • Quarterly training
KPIs: Retention Rate, Training Effectiveness

💰 Finance & Accounts

  • Daily revenue reconciliation
  • Monthly audits
  • Expense tracking
KPIs: Revenue Accuracy, Cost Control
